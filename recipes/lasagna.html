Lasagna:

Prep Time: 30 mins Cook Time: 2 hrs 30 mins Additional Time: 15 mins Total Time: 3 hrs 15 mins Servings: 12

Ingredients

  • 1lb. Ground Beef, 1lb. Ground Lean Italian Sausage

  • 2 cups Yellow Onion, Diced

  • 1/2 cup Fresh Garlic, Finely Chopped

  • 1 can Crushed Tomatoes, 2 cans Tomato Sauce, 2 cans Tomato Paste

  • 2 tbsp White Sugar

  • Herbs and Spices: Fresh Parsely, Dried Basil, Italian Seasoning, Fennel Seeds, Salt, and Black Pepper

  • Lasagna Noodles

  • Cheeses: Parmesan, Mozzerella, and Ricotta

  • 1 Egg

Instructions:

  1. Prepare meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Mix egg with ricotta.
  4. Build lasagna according to sequence below.

Layering Sequence:

  1. Sauce

  2. Noodles

  3. Ricotta

  4. Mozzarrella slices

  5. Sauce

  6. Parmesan

  7. Repeat

Cooking:

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees fahrenheit.

  2. Cover lasagna with aluminum foil.

  3. Bake for 25 min.

  4. Remove foil.

  5. Bake an additional 25 min.

  6. Allow to rest for 15-20 mins before serving.
